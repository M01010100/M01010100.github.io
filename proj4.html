Self Hosting a Hackathon

A Project & Challange based hackathon for students


Preface

As a student, I have always wanted to host a hackathon. I have participated in a few hackathons and have always enjoyed the experience and noticed a gap in the market. There are not many Computer Science based hackathons in the Central New York Region There are competions hosted within schools but none that strive to be a collective body of students competing. Something similar to the CNY Hackathon. This is where I will denote that were I attend college in Utica-Rome area a Hackathon denotes a Cybersecurity competition. I learned that there was a CS rendition of the CNY but for reasons unkown in addition to the pandemic it has ceased to exist. I intend to reserect it in some form.
