Short Biography

I am currently attending SUNY Polytechnic Institue and will be graduating in December 2024 with a BS in Network Computer Security with a Minor in Computer Science. I plan to continue my education at SUNY Polytechnic to earn a Masters in Computer Science. My interests include Machine Learning, Computer hardware, and Cryptography. At SUNY Poly I am the president of the NCS club on campus for fall 2024. As the club president I am currently overseeing the development of a lab, creation of infrastructure for the club and extended development of the clubs internal hackathon program. I have competed at several hackathons such as the NCAE Cybergames (2022-2023) and the CNY Hackathon (2019-2021-2023-2024) and in the CNY I recieved the MVP award for team 7 in 2023. That was for overall team leadership and maintaing the teams Database infrastucture.Beyond the "Cyber" Hackathons I have also competed in several project-based hackathons such as Gatehacks (2024), where my team placed 3rd overall with our AI ASL interpreter.
