Short Biography

I am currently attending SUNY Polytechnic Institue as a Computer Science Graduate Student studying the intersections of Machine Learning, Quantum Computing, and Cybersecurity. I have a Bachelor's degree in Network Computer Security from SUNY Polytechnic Institue and an Associates in Computer Science: Cybersecurity from MVCC At SUNY Poly I am the president of the NCS club on campus for the 2024-2025 academic year. As the club president, I am currently overseeing the development of a lab, the procurment of infrastructure (includes servers for VMs, storage, and AI), and extended development of the club's internal hackathon program. In addtion to my leadership of the NCS club I was asked to join the CS Club Leadership. I have competed at several hackathons such as the NCAE Cybergames (2022-2023) and the CNY Hackathon (2019-2021-2023-2024) and in the CNY I received the MVP award for team 7 in 2023. That was for overall team leadership and maintaining the team's Database infrastructure. Beyond the "Cyber" Hackathons I have also competed in several project-based hackathons such as Gatehacks (2024), where my team placed 3rd overall with our AI ASL interpreter.
